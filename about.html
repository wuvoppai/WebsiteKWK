Know your host

Krishnansh Sharma

Krishnansh Sharma is the podcaster and the host of the show Konvo With Krishnansh. He started off as an impersonator (mimicry artist) and a theatre actor. Throughout this journey, he had a keen interest towards the audio format content. He has acted in various short films with well-known Youtube Channels with a subscriber base of around 400k subscribers. He has also played various major roles in theatre plays created by The Craftomaniacs, Official Theatre Society of Inderprastha Engineering College.

A message from the podcaster

Podcasting to me is what playing with toys to a toddler is. With that being said, I don't intend to mean that it is just so simple for me, no it's not. But it means that it is so enjoyable for me that I can do it for hours without a break. I have always been a guy interested in conversations related to various sorts of topics ranging from as serious as geopolitics & spirituality to as casual as my engineering! XD

This is a dream that I am living currently. I have always been into content creation via various means; be it as an Instagram Reel creator, or a youtuber. But I was yet to find my domain, my niche. And then thankfully I started listening podcasts by Ranveer Allahbadia, Vinamre Kasana & Joe Rogan. Damn! That was the moment I decided that let's give it a shot. Another thing that motivated me to begin podcasting was the shower of appreciative compliments from my friends about my voice. I started off my podcasting journey and then rest is all in front of you! Thankyou for reading this... Have a lovely day ahead! Cheers! :)
